LoLCooldown

Welcome to LoLCooldown!

This is a brief tour to showcase what LoLCooldown can do.

If you want to access this window later, press in the header of the first window.


  • In-Game & Champ-Select Cooldowns: The cooldowns of summoner spells & champion abilities will be displayed during champ-select & a live game for both teams. Cooldowns for active items are displayed as well upon purchase. Cooldown Reduction is put in mind while calculating the cooldowns (items, runes, and Hexteck drake)


    I, S, B, and U show the Items, Summoner Spells, Basic abilities, and Ultimate ability Cooldown reduction respectively. Hover over them to see the items used to inflence them



  • Abilities that decreases cooldowns: An icon will appear next to an ability to signify that the ability decreases cooldown (eg. Ezreal Q, Annie Q). Hovering over the ability to get more details.
  • Summoner Spell, Ultimate , Active item timer: Press on a summoner spell, an ultimate, or an active item to start a timer. The ultimate time is chosen dependant on the champion's level assuming that the player will prioritize ranking up the ultimate (e.g. lvl 6: rank 1, lvl 11: rank 2). When it's done, you will be notified by an alert (if you wanted). Check Settings for the available options. You can right click on a timer to reduce it by 5 seconds.

  • Basic and Advanced modes: What have you seen thus far is the advanced mode; if you are only interested in using the timers, then you can switch to basic mode in the settings to show the timers for the enemy team in-game.



That's all!
